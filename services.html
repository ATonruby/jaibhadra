Services

Jaibhadra Group works with the Governments, business owners and senior management toward achieving their goals and objectives by providing financial and strategic advice. Jaibhadra Group specializes in Government to Government tie ups and Government to Business tie ups across sectors. Through its experienced team and its deep understanding of the business and Governmental environment, it is well positioned to provide comprehensive solutions taking into account all aspects of the business.

Strategic

  • Corporate Structuring including creation of appropriate business, holding and borrowing entities.
  • Advice on expansion strategies including growth in new markets and geographies.
  • Market Entry & Exit Strategy.

Financial

  • Preparation of business plans and financial projections.
  • Setting up of robust in-house finance function.

Strategic

  • Management of Currency and Interest Rate exposures.
  • Liquidity Management.
  • Establishment of appropriate financial risk mitigation process.

We work very closely with the Policy Makers, Opinion Leaders, Bureaucracy, and Owners of Businesses to draw up a practical framework and assist in implementing the ideas. Jaibhadra Group has successfully partnered some of the leading companies for their India operations with well networked strategies in Government at both levels of Bureaucracy & Political Leadership.
