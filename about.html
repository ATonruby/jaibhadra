About us

Jaibhadra Group is a strategic advisory firm that provides tailor-made strategic solutions to its clients. Our team is enriched with domestic Indian regulatory frameworks and cultural sensitivities which helps us attain quick conclusion of deals. At Jaibhadra we help our Clients better capitalize on their ideas in the fast moving local and global markets. We blend our approach and ability with unique access to vast geographic and industry resources. Jaibhadra Group works with companies that have the desire to grow. Jaibhadra advises and specializes on Crisis Management solutions relating to strategic matters.

Jaibhadra offers its services in the field of Financial, Regulatory, Compliance and Strategic Advisory. Jaibhadra believes in advising business owners and senior management in strategic matters to help improve performance and achieve their goals.

Jaibhadra Group consists of an experienced team that has worked together for over a decade and have been instrumental in managing and executing large deals. The team has a solid track record of successful transactions across sectors. The team is well complemented by senior industry leaders and experts who bring to bear deep operating experience. The team brings diverse and deep multi-functional business experience to deliver desired returns.

At Jaibhadra, we seek to develop and maintain focused, have long-term relationships with the management of a highly select group of companies. By concentrating our efforts and resources on a select group of companies in these primary industries, we achieve a high level of expertise and understanding. This expertise and understanding coupled with a close working relationship with management allows us to deliver on the mission of identifying the best opportunities.
